The Plan

Bangkok & Pattaya Itinerary

Four days. Memorise it. Then embrace the chaos.

Day 1 · Thursday 30 April

Bangkok Arrival

The calm before the storm

Evening

Welcome Rooftop Drinks

Bangkok · 49 Floors Up

Welcome drinks 49 floors above Bangkok with views that make you feel like you've earned something — even though you've just been on a plane. Everyone arrives, drinks get ordered, and somebody suggests "just a quiet one tonight." The group nods. Nobody means it. Get there early for a table.

🥂 Group Gathering
Bangkok · Night 1
Night

Drinks Around Sukhumvit

Bangkok

A few bars around the strip. A "relaxed" warm-up night that quietly turns into 2am. Classic. The real activities start tomorrow — or so you'll keep telling yourself.

🍺 Easy Night (Allegedly)

Day 2 · Friday 1 May

The Big One

From karts to clubs. Godspeed.

10:00am

Topkart Bangkok

Highest Rated Track in Bangkok

4.9 stars. Long track, fast karts, absolutely zero sympathy for the groom. Whoever wins buys the first round. Whoever crashes (James) buys two. Book ahead — group slots go fast and nobody wants to spend the morning in a queue instead of a kart.

🏎️ Pre-Book Required
12:00pm

Lunch

Nearby

Refuel. Someone will be walking like they aged 30 years from the karts. Someone else will insist they "went easy." Neither is true. Eat something substantial — tonight is long.

Bangkok · Refuel
2:00pm

Free Time

Hotel / Pool / Explore

Pool, hotel room, or a wander. Nap if you need to. You won't admit you napped, but you will nap.

5:00pm

Sunset Rooftop

45 Floors Up · 360° Views

45 floors up. Bangkok stretches out beneath you. The sunset turns everything gold. Arrive right on opening to guarantee a table — it fills instantly and standing up here staring at someone else's seats is not the vibe. This is the group photo that makes everyone back home deeply envious. Take it.

🌅 Arrive at Opening
7:30pm

Quilombo Bangkok

Thong Lo · Argentine Steakhouse

Argentine steakhouse in a dramatic castle-like venue. One of the best-reviewed restaurants in Bangkok. Steak, wine, speeches that are only slightly too personal. Book a table in advance — it fills up, and standing outside a castle waiting to eat is nobody's idea of a stag do.

🥩 Book in Advance
10:00pm

BACCARAT

Sukhumvit 24 · The Main Event

House and electronic music, strong DJs, a crowd that knows how to go. Reserve a table ahead — standing at the bar all night is not the vibe. Dress well enough to get in. Don't worry about staying that way.

🎧 Reserve a Table
Bangkok · The Night Doesn't End Here
Late

Night Ends When It Ends

Wherever Bangkok Takes You

No curfew. No promises. No scheduled end time. You'll know when it's over.

Day 3 · Saturday 3 May

Bangkok → Pattaya

New city. Same energy.

Morning

Slow Start

Hotel

Late checkout. Strong coffee. Nobody talks until everyone is fed. Collective silence is respected. Pack your bags.

☕ Earned It
Bangkok · The Morning After
11am–12pm

Minibus to Pattaya

~2 Hours · Bangkok → Pattaya

Chartered minibus departs around 11–12. Nap en route. Mandatory snacks. Someone will attempt to watch a film on their phone without headphones. Deal with it collectively.

Pattaya · New City. New Rules.
Afternoon

Beach Club

Pattaya

Check in. Freshen up. Head straight to a beach club — pool, sun, cold drinks. The decompression phase. Scientifically necessary after two nights in Bangkok.

Evening

Dinner Near Walking Street

Pattaya

Proper food before the night kicks off. You've learned your lesson from Day 2. Or you haven't. Either way, eat.

Night

Walking Street

Pattaya's Main Strip

Bar crawl down the strip to find your bearings, then pick a home base. High energy, great DJs, strong reviews. What happens on Walking Street stays in Walking Street. This is not a rule, it's a survival strategy.

🌃 Pattaya Night
Walking Street, Pattaya

Day 4 · Sunday 4 May

The Final Chapter

Everything you've got, one last time.

Morning

Breakfast & Rehydration

Hotel

Nobody is moving fast. Order everything on the breakfast menu. Electrolytes are your friend. Don't ask how anyone's feeling.

11:00am

Quad Biking

ATV & Buggy Adventures, Pattaya

4.8 stars and nearly 2,000 reviews of people doing exactly what you're about to do. Book the 2-hour session — the shorter one gets mixed reviews and you'll want the full experience. If anyone falls off a quad, the story gets told at the wedding speech. No exceptions. Pre-book as group slots fill fast.

🏍️ Pre-Book Required
1:30pm

Back to Hotel

Rest & Recover

Shower. Lie flat. Scroll your phone. Tell yourself you're fine. You're fine.

4:00pm

Sunset Rooftop

34th Floor · Pattaya Bay Views

Best views in Pattaya, across the bay to the horizon. Happy hour kicks in before sunset — arrive early for the best table AND the buy-one-get-one deal. This is the "we're actually going to miss each other" moment. Don't tell anyone.

🌅 Happy Hour · BOGO Drinks
Pattaya · 34th Floor, Sunset Drinks
7:00pm

Final Group Dinner

Make It Count

The last supper. The real speeches happen here — the ones that are too honest, too long, and finish to applause and one person wiping their eye. James pays for his own drinks. This is not negotiable.

🍾 Speeches Encouraged
9:30pm

Walking Street — Final Night

Pattaya

Go later. Stay later. Whatever is left of you after four days, put it all here. James gets married in eight days. This is your closing argument for his single life. Make it count.
